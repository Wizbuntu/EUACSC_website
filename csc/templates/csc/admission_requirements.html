Admission Requirements

Admission Requirements for computer science department.

UTME Entry

In addition to the University entry requirement, candidate for our programme must have credit in English Language, Mathematics, Physics and any two subjects from Chemistry, Biology, Further Mathematics and Statistics in WASC, SSCE, GCE, NECO or their equivalent examinations in at most two sittings.

Direct Entry (DE)

Direct entry candidates also do four year program in Computer Science Department but direct entry candidate who possess the Diploma in Statistics and Computer Application may be considered, on individual merit, for a waiver of some courses..
